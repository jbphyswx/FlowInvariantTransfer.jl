FlowEnergyTransfer.jl
Energy transfer from scale to scale (or computationally feasibly, shell to shell)


Supports both methods for across scale transfer estimates (direct, coarse-graining, structure functions, etc),
and methods for scale-to-scale energy transfer estimates.


For scale to scale energy transfer estimates inspiration, see:
- Local shell-to-shell energy transfer via nonlocal Interactions in fluid turbulence [https://arxiv.org/abs/nlin/0204027]
- Cross-Scale Surface Kinetic Energy Transfer of Ocean Mesoscale Eddies in the Southern Ocean Revealed from the SWOT Mission [https://doi.org/10.1175/JPO-D-24-0134.1] [https://github.com/wsp0422ocean/shell-to-shell-modes/tree/main] [https://github.com/wsp0422ocean/shell-to-shell-modes/blob/main/shell-to-shell_energy_transfer.py]
